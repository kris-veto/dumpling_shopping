History of Gyoza

Gyoza, a beloved food in Japan, believed to be introduced from China in the 19th Century. Its popularity among Japanese sky rocketed after World War II, while returning solidiers from northern-east of China was craving for the iconic street food.

Today, gyoza is a staple part of Japanese cuisine, readily found in ramen shops, Chinese restaurants, specialty stores, and even convenience stores.

Gyoza actually has so many forms all around the world.
